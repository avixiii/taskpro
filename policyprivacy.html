Task Management - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 31, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Task Management! This Privacy Policy outlines how Task Management ("we," "our," or "us") handles your privacy when using our app. We are dedicated to safeguarding your privacy and want to inform you that we do not collect any personal information. This policy explains what information we don't collect and how we ensure your privacy is protected.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Task Management is designed with your privacy as a priority. We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) from our users. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Usernames
  • Email addresses
  • Contact information
  • Financial information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers

3. Data Usage

Task Management does not process, store, or transmit any personal data from its users. The app's sole purpose is to provide real-time cryptocurrency price information sourced from CoinGecko, without accessing, tracking, or collecting any user-related data.

4. Advertisements

Task Management is an ad-free application. We do not display third-party advertisements and, as a result, do not engage in any user data collection for ad targeting purposes.

5. Third-Party Integrations

The app integrates with the CoinGecko API to fetch live cryptocurrency market data. Please note that your interactions with CoinGecko through Task Management are subject to CoinGecko's Privacy Policy. We recommend reviewing their policy to understand how they manage data.

6. Security

We are committed to maintaining the security of our app and its users. Task Management employs industry-standard security measures to protect the app. However, since we do not collect any personal information, there is no risk of such data being compromised.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect any changes in our data practices. Any significant updates will be communicated to users, and the latest version of the policy will always be available within the Task Management app.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or the Task Management app, please contact us at support@avixiii.com.

Thank you for choosing Task Management. Your privacy and trust are our top priorities.
